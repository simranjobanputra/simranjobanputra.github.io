About Me

My name is Simran Jobanputra and I'm a current student at Carnegie Mellon pursuing a Masters in Human Computer Interaction. In May 2019, I also graduated with my bachelors in Business and Human Computer Interaction from Carnegie Mellon. I recently interned at NASA AMES as a UX Designer, where I worked on an application called Playbook in order to create timesaving features and shortcuts for astronauts.

Growing up as a first-generation Indian American in the city of Chicago, I have always embraced being a part of two completely distinct perspectives in order to solve a complicated puzzle. For this reason, I'm passionate about coming up with innovative solutions that can serve the needs of many different communities. Outside of the classroom, I'm super involved in the dance community and even started my own team, CMU Sahara. I also loved getting involved in entrepreneurship and building out new ideas.

Please feel free to reach out to me to collaborate or give any feedback at sjobanpu@cs.cmu.edu!
