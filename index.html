About Me

My name is Simran Jobanputra and I'm a current student at Carnegie Mellon pursuing a Masters in Human Computer Interaction. In May 2019, I also graduated with my bachelors in Business and Human Computer Interaction from Carnegie Mellon. I recently interned at NASA AMES as a UX Designer, where I worked on an application called Playbook in order to create timesaving features and shortcuts for astronauts.

Growing up as a first-generation Indian American in the city of Chicago, I have always embraced being a part of two completely distinct perspectives in order to solve a complicated puzzle. For this reason, I'm passionate about coming up with innovative solutions that can serve the needs of many different communities.
