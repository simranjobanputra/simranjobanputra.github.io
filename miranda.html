Project Ask

Design a conversational user interface to enhance the retail experience.

Project Overview

This project was part of my Advanced Interaction Design studio. The main goal was to better understand chatbots and conversational user interfaces and how they could contribute to improving problems in the retail experience, especially given the massive shift to online shopping. A big question for my team was understanding how we could reinvogorate people to shop in stores.
