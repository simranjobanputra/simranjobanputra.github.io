Impact

02 Solution

The convenience of online shopping has stagnated the number of shoppers coming into stores and malls. Our goal was two-fold:

1) to get more shoppers in stores
2) to make the shopping experience more enjoyable



Miranda is a conversational user interface that reinvigorates the joy of in-store shopping by building an experience that elevates department stores - personalizing the shopping experience for the customer and making inventory management and customer service easier for floor associates.

The CUI serves as a "front stage" manager as shoppers peruse the storefront while the stylist is the "backstage manager" in the fitting and trials area.
