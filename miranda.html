02 Solution

The convenience of online shopping has stagnated the number of in-store shoppers. Our goal was two-fold:

  • to get more shoppers in stores

  • to make the shopping experience more enjoyable



Miranda is a conversational user interface that reinvigorates the joy of in-store shopping by building an experience that elevates department stores.

  • personalizing the shopping experience for the customer

  • making inventory management + customer service easier for floor associates

03 Approach

Research

Voice-based Competitor Analysis

Prior to building a CUI, our team took a deep dive into the current market and features that make products stand out over others.
We looked at chatbots and in-home devices, such as Alexa and Siri.






Ethnographic Research to Identify Opportunities
Our team split up and spoke with customers at malls and customers in the process of making online shopping decisions to understand where in-store shopping is lacking.



Current CUIs in Retail
Many retailers have already begun to employ CUIs to solve the types of problems exposed in our ethnographic research.
