02 Challenge

Project Ask

Design a base station interface to increase efficiency and reduce cognitive overload for the base station operator as they monitor multiple air and ground robots while conducting artifact detection.

Project Overview

This project was my Undergraduate HCI Capstone. Our client was Team Explorer, which consists of 20+ students and faculty in the CMU Robotics Institute and Oregon State University’s Robotic Decision Making Lab. Our goal was to help them succeed in the DARPA Subterranean Challenge, a multi-year competition to rapidly map, navigate and search underground environments using semi-autonomous ground and aerial robots. The underlying goal of the competition is to ultimately support emergency first responders and combat operators in complex underground environments.

Impact

We helped the team increase their efficiency in detections by 5-10%. Additionally, Team Explorer placed first in the first round of the competition.
