Project Ask

Typically, astronauts follow mission plans given to them by mission control. This leads astronauts to be dependent on mission control when experiments or activities need to be changed. Playbook is an intuitive software designed to help astronauts plan certain adjustable activities in space or on other analog missions, without needing to reach out to mission control everytime. This has a useful application in missions where there is a time lag between mission control (Earth) and the astronauts who may be on the Moon or Mars. Currently, this software is used on analog missions(field tests in locations that have physical similarities to extreme space environments) such as NASA NEEMO, but the hope is our software can be used for Mars 2020 and the like!
